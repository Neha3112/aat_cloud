What We Do

  • The Company Prepares the Inventory list or E-Waste list Designated for disposal to Government Authorized E-Waste Recycling Company.
  • The E-Waste Recycling Company Inspects the Materials and Quotes The price.
  • Out of 3 or more quotes, the company decides to dispose of the e-waste to Highest Bidder with the highest compliance standards.
  • The recycler will Perform the activities such as Data Degaussing, Data sanitization, etc (Onsite) if itâ€™s part of the work order / E-Waste Disposal.
  • GST Invoice (For E-Waste 5%) Generation by the company and issues to Recycler.
  • The recycler will make payment against the invoice copy received.
  • Loading of materials (photo capture during loading) and GPS tracking as per pollution control board will be followed by the Recycler.
  • The recycler will issue Form 6 (Transport Manifest ) and Passbook entry will be done during the moment of material from company to recycling facility
  • Capturing the images during unloading at the recycling facility
  • Segregation, Dismantling, and Recycling of commodities.
  • Issuance of Green Certificate, Asset Destruction Certificate, Data Degaussing Certificate, Data sanitization certificate in 15 to 45 days as per the recycling industry standards
